# Academic Humanizer Test Cases

## Test 1 — Technical Accuracy

### Objective
Verify that technical terminology is preserved.

### Pass Criteria
- No technical terms changed.
- No numerical values changed.
- No equations changed.

---

## Test 2 — Citation Integrity

### Objective
Verify that citations remain attached to their supporting statements.

### Pass Criteria
- No citations removed.
- Citation order unchanged.
- Citation formatting preserved.

---

## Test 3 — AI Pattern Removal

### Objective
Verify that identified AI-associated writing patterns are eliminated where appropriate.

### Pass Criteria
- Repetitive sentence openings removed.
- Formulaic phrases revised.
- Repetitive transitions reduced.
- Natural sentence variation introduced.

---

## Test 4 — Publication Quality

### Objective
Determine whether the revised text is suitable for journal submission.

### Pass Criteria
- Natural academic tone.
- Logical flow.
- No unnecessary verbosity.
- No unsupported claims introduced.
